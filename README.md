# Customer Churn Analysis and Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project analyzes customer behavior and develops a machine learning model to identify customers who are likely to leave the service.

The objective is to help businesses proactively reduce churn by understanding customer characteristics and predicting future churn.

---
## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn leads to revenue loss and increased customer acquisition costs. Without identifying at-risk customers early, companies struggle to implement effective retention strategies.

This project aims to:

Identify the factors that contribute to customer churn.
Analyze customer demographics and service usage.
Build a predictive model to classify customers as likely to churn or stay.
Generate actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.
